# Gregory Hale

## Overview
Gregory Hale is Samuel’s father. A hard working hull sector laborer with a violent temper worsened by alcoholism. Sober, he was kind and charming. Drunk, he was unpredictable and terrifying. Samuel is the only child who remembers the good parts.

## Core Traits
- Strong, stubborn, volatile
- Charismatic when sober
- Emotionally damaged
- Haunted by shame

## Strengths
- Reliable provider
- Skilled laborer
- Loved deeply in his rare healthy moments

## Flaws
- Violent temper
- Alcohol dependence
- Unable to express feelings safely
- Feared by most of his children

## Emotional Wound
Crushed by a mixture of generational trauma, isolation, and life on the ship. Alcohol was the only relief he allowed himself.

## Relationship Notes
- **Samuel:** Firstborn. The only one who saw the sober father.
- **Ruth:** Loved him and suffered because of him.
- **Other children:** Remember only the terror.

## Arc Notes
- Dies early.
- Leaves a complicated legacy that shapes Samuel’s worldview.
