# Helen Marlow

## Overview
Helen Marlow is Lydia's mother. She supported Peter in missionary work, raised Lydia with strict but gentle expectations, and never understood why her daughter fled. Loved by many, misunderstood by one.

## Core Traits
- Kind and devout
- Duty driven
- Patient but rigid
- Blind to emotional nuance

## Strengths
- Steady presence
- Supportive partner
- Organized and thorough

## Flaws
- Emotionally tone-deaf
- Places faith above personal needs
- Unintentionally dismissive of Lydia’s pain

## Emotional Wound
Her identity is built around service and faith. Cannot process that these same values harmed her daughter.

## Relationship Notes
- **Lydia:** Loves her but sees her choices as betrayal.
- **Peter:** A united front in ministry work.
- **Jonah:** Welcoming, loving, and unaware of his inner turmoil.

## Arc Notes
- Key figure in Lydia’s trauma.
- Embodiment of a faith that helps some and wounds others.
