# Peter Marlow

## Overview
Peter Marlow is Lydia's father. A missionary on the ship who, with his wife, devoted their lives to spreading the Gospel. He is warm, charismatic, and beloved by everyone except the daughter he unintentionally hurt.

## Core Traits
- Friendly and energetic
- Deeply committed to faith
- Strong sense of mission
- Well liked and respected

## Strengths
- Great communicator
- Warm and approachable
- A natural leader in faith communities

## Flaws
- Emotionally absent father
- Prioritized saving others over loving his own child
- Blind to the harm caused by strict religious life

## Emotional Wound
Believes wholeheartedly he served God. Cannot see what he did wrong with Lydia.

## Relationship Notes
- **Lydia:** Loves her but never understood her rebellion.
- **Helen:** Ministry partner and emotional backbone.
- **Jonah:** Jonah sees the good man behind the harm. It destroys him.

## Arc Notes
- Symbol of religious contradiction.
- Catalyst for Lydia’s rebellion and Jonah’s confusion.
