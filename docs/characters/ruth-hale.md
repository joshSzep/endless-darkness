# Ruth Hale

## Overview
Ruth Hale is Samuel’s mother. A devout Christian who raised six children with tenderness and quiet strength while married to a volatile husband. She is the emotional heart of the Hale family.

## Core Traits
- Soft-spoken
- Devout and sincere
- Steady under pressure
- Deeply compassionate

## Strengths
- Emotional resilience
- Nurturing presence
- Strong moral compass
- Community trust

## Flaws
- Endured too much without boundaries
- Normalized emotional suppression
- Over-relied on faith instead of seeking help

## Emotional Wound
Living with a man she loved who became dangerous when drunk. Her faith became both a refuge and a coping mechanism.

## Relationship Notes
- **Samuel:** Her rock. She trusted him too early.
- **Gregory:** Loved him despite the harm.
- **Grandchildren:** They all adore her.

## Arc Notes
- Provides the emotional backbone of Samuel’s identity.
- Represents old Earth faith that survives across generations.
