# Sera Solano

## Overview
Sera Solano becomes the love of Jonah’s life. Curious, grounded, steady under pressure. She sees Jonah clearly, without fear or false optimism. She takes the lead early but never tries to fix him.

## Core Traits
- Curious and thoughtful
- Patient and observant
- Emotionally mature
- Loyal without being self-sacrificing
- Has strong boundaries

## Strengths
- Reads people well
- Holds steady during emotional storms
- Comfort with silence
- Practical mindset
- Brave in subtle ways

## Flaws
- Hesitates to ask for help
- Can compartmentalize too much
- Sometimes carries her own pain quietly

## Emotional Wound
Comes from a family that is stable but closed off. Learned young that understanding someone deeply is rare and precious.

## Relationship Notes
- **Jonah:** She sees the real him from the start.
- **Samuel:** He approves of her immediately.
- **Lydia:** Understands her sadness but never gets close.

## Arc Notes
- Becomes Jonah’s anchor.
- Plays a critical role during his crisis.
- Helps him choose connection over isolation.
