# Economy

## Overview
The economy is a managed system. There is no currency in the traditional sense. Distribution is based on need, contribution, and long term stability.

## Resource Management
- AI optimized distribution
- Human oversight councils
- Strict recycling and reclamation
- Balance between population and resource load

## Labor Structure
- Essential roles: engineering, agriculture, medical, hull maintenance
- Secondary roles: education, administration, arts
- Career assignments shaped by ability and societal need

## Trade
Within the ship:
- Goods traded through barter or allocation requests
Across generations:
- Skill traditions passed down by family or guild

## Relevance to Jonah
His family sits in the working class. Samuel’s job is essential but not prestigious. This shapes Jonah’s early sense of identity.
